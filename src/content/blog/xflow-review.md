## What is Xflow?

Xflow India is designed for businesses working with global clients, exporters, digital agencies, freelancers and startups. Its core promise is making international payments fast, secure and transparent. Instead of slow, opaque bank wires, Xflow provides:

- **Virtual international bank accounts:** Overseas clients pay into local-like accounts, boosting success rates and convenience.

- **Live FX rates with no hidden spread:** You see exactly how much INR you'll get. Xflow uses mid-market rates without secret fees.

- **Automated FIRA (Foreign Inward Remittance Advice):** Digital compliance receipts are issued on every transaction, eliminating manual paperwork.

- **Faster INR settlements:** Funds reach your Indian account quicker than standard multi-day bank transfers.

## Xflow Review

##### Key Features of Xflow

- **Virtual Multi-Currency Accounts:** Xflow gives Indian firms virtual accounts in dollars, euros, etc. Clients pay like locals, avoiding SWIFT. This cuts costs and delays.

- **Real-Time FX & No Markup:** You get the live interbank rate. It does not charge any FX markup and converts foreign currency at live FX rates. You know exactly what lands in INR before transfer.

- **Digital FIRA Issuance:** Xflow automatically generates the required GST-compliance eFIRA for each receipt. This removes tedious back-and-forth with banks or accountants.

- **Quick Settlements:** Instead of 3–5 day SWIFT delays, Xflow settles funds in 24 hours or so. Faster money = smoother cash flow for SMEs.

- **API and White-Label Options:** (For platforms) Xflow can also be embedded via APIs, allowing software companies to offer global payments under their own brand.

#### Xflow Pros & Cons

##### Pros

Using Xflow has clear upsides:

- **Transparent, Predictable Fees:** Xflow's pricing is flat and upfront. Xflow charges about 1% per transaction with no hidden bank fees. Unlike banks, you see the fee and rate in advance.

- **Fair FX Rates:** You get live market rates with no concealed margin. This transparency helps avoid revenue loss from sneaky bank conversions.

- **Automated Compliance:** Digital FIRAs and GST paperwork are handled automatically. Businesses avoid chasing the bank for papers, saving time on audits or refunds.

- **Faster Receipts:** Compared to standard SWIFT, Xflow's transfers settle much quicker. Quicker USD/EUR→INR means better cash flow.

##### Cons

However, Xflow has some limitations to consider:

- **Not a Full Bank:** Xflow is focused on receipts (collections). It doesn't replace a bank account or offer loans, cards, or expense management. You'll still need banking or other fintech for payments, credit, or corporate cards.

- **Narrow Ecosystem:** It excels at inbound payments only. If you want expense tracking, payroll, cards or an all-in-one ERP, you'll need extra tools. Xflow specializes in collections, so additional platforms might be needed for end-to-end finances.

#### Xflow Pricing

On each inbound payment, Xflow takes about 1% of the amount. For instance, receiving $10,000 might incur $100 in fees.

They convert the currency at the mid-market rate. There's no additional spread beyond the 1%.
